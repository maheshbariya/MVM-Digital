What is digital marketing and why does my business need it?

Digital marketing uses online platforms like Google, social media, email, and websites to reach potential customers. It helps businesses build visibility, generate leads, and increase sales in a cost-effective way.

How is digital marketing different from traditional marketing?

Traditional marketing (print, TV, radio) reaches a mass audience but lacks precise targeting. Digital marketing allows you to target specific audiences, track performance in real-time, and optimize campaigns for better ROI.
